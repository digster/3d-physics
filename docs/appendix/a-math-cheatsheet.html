Appendix A

Math Cheat-Sheet

Every formula the engine leans on, in one place. Each row links back to the chapter that explains the why; this page is just the what. Notation: a·b is the dot product, a×b the cross product, |a| a length, and a hat (a^) a unit vector.

Vectors (Ch 2)

QuantityFormulaMeaning
Dot product a·b=axbx+ayby+azbz=|a||b|cosθ Alignment; 0 = perpendicular.
Cross product a×b=(aybz−azby,azbx−axbz,axby−aybx) Perpendicular vector; length = area.
Length |a|=a·a Compare with a·a to skip the root.
Normalize a^=a/|a| Keep the direction, set length to 1.
Projection projba=a·bb·bb The shadow of a along b.
Reflection r=v−2(v·n^)n^ Mirror bounce about a surface normal.
